Addition and Subtraction
========================

## `add a, b`
`a += b`

`a` and `b` can't both be memory.

## `sub a, b`
`a -= b`

`a` and `b` can't both be memory.

## `inc a`
`a++`

## `dec a`
`a--`

Multiplication and Division
===========================

## `mul a`
`eax:edx = eax*a`

## `div a`
`eax, edx = divmod(eax:edx, a)`
